Whale Watching with a Toddler

Elding (eldingwhalewatching.is) accepts under-4 free, 7–15 reduced. The tour is 2.5–3 hrs on the open Atlantic — pack thermal layers and waterproofs, snacks, and a carrier for cabin breaks. Pick a calm-weather morning. If the tour cancels, swap straight into Laugardalur for the morning instead.

Sneaker Waves

Keep the toddler FAR from wave shores at Reynisfjara and Diamond Beach. Sneaker waves are dangerously unpredictable — hold hands at all times.

Car Seat & Midnight Sun

Book a car seat with your rental in advance. Late May has midnight sun — pack a sleep mask or blackout setup for the toddler.

Toddler Travel Kit

Bring a soft carrier (Ergobaby) in addition to a stroller — many paths are uneven. Pack waterproofs and warm layers; May is 5–12°C.

Petrol Station Pit Stops

Icelandic petrol stations (N1, Olis) serve toddler-approved hot dogs and soup — your go-to fuel + bathroom stop on long drive days.

Iceland Tap Water

Tap water is among the best in the world and perfectly safe — fill bottles everywhere instead of buying.

Spotting Baby Lambies

Sheep roam freely in May — spotting "baby lambies" from the car is pure toddler magic. Make a game of counting them on long drive days.

Whale Watching Tour

Book the whale watching tour on Sep 4 now while the schedule is still flexible. This is the best day to secure a morning departure from Reykjavík or nearby coastal ports.

Travel Tip

Keep Sep 6 open for the Westman Islands and allow extra time for ferry schedules; weather can shift quickly in early September.
